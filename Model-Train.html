Six-Domain Judicial Expertise

1) Issue Spotting

Identifies the core legal questions and disputes before the court.

2) Principle Extraction

Accurately distills the ratio decidendi (binding legal principle) from judgments.

3) Precedent Analysis

Analyzes the application of case law (distinguishing, following, or overruling).

4) Judgment Structuring

Produces structured legal outlines (Facts, Issue, Analysis, Order).

5) Outcome Prediction

Determines the probable court decision or extracts the final binding order.

6) Legal Summarisation

Creates comprehensive and nuanced case summaries and synthesis.
